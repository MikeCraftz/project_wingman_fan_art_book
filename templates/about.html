About the Institute

United Services Institute(USI) is the oldest and leading academic institution dedicated to the study of conflict, security and geopolitics. Since our foundation in AC292 by the Decree of Parliament, USI had continously produced in-depth analyses on emerging military issues, defence management, procurement and doctrine for policies maker and militar planners.

"De Re Militari" is a seasonal publication of the United Services Institute. It is a collection of articles, analysis, and commentary on the emerging military issues and trends.

Mission Statement

To analyse, educate, and preserve the lessons of defense, security and geopolitics for the benefit of the global community.
